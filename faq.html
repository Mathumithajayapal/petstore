Orders & Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the contiguous United States. Expedited shipping options (2-day and overnight) are available at checkout for an additional fee. You will receive a tracking link via email as soon as your order ships.
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $49. The subtotal must reach $49 after all discounts and coupons have been applied. Heavy items, such as large bags of dog food, may incur a slight heavy-weight surcharge which will be clearly indicated at checkout.
Currently, PetHaven only ships within the United States, including Alaska and Hawaii. We are looking to expand into Canada and Mexico by the end of the year. Subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to know when international shipping opens up!

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you or your pet are not 100% satisfied with a product, you can return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund to your original payment method. The item must be in mostly new condition (e.g., unopened toys, barely used food bags).
Yes! We call this our "Picky Eater Guarantee." If your pet turns their nose up at their new kibble, you can return the open bag for a store credit or full refund as long as the bag is still at least 75% full. We usually donate the remaining food to local animal shelters.

Product Information

The majority of our premium treat brands are grain-free, all-natural, and organic. However, we also carry specialized vet-recommended treats that include healthy grains. You can always use the "Grain-Free" filter on our product pages to narrow down your search.
Every apparel product page includes a specific Sizing Chart. We recommend measuring your dog's neck, chest girth (the widest part behind the front legs), and back length. If your dog is between sizes, we always recommend sizing up for comfort.
